In case of having a CESA obligation, will the amount be withheld when withdrawing funds from the game account via a bank card?

Yes, in case of having a CESA obligation, when funds are withdrawn from the game account via a bank card, the amount of the given obligation is blocked by the Bank, and the remaining part of the amount can be managed by the customer at his/her discretion.

For example, if the customer has a CESA obligation of 3,000 AMD and withdraws 10,000 AMD from the game account, the Bank will block 3,000 AMD from the transferred 10,000 AMD, and the customer can manage the remaining 7,000 AMD at his/her discretion.

If the withdrawal amount is less than the obligation amount, the Bank blocks the entire amount transferred to the bank card.
